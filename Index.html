Adhocsy – PILOT Run Request

On-Demand Workforce Platform | Proof of Concept (PoC)

1. About Adhocsy

Adhocsy is a technology-led platform enabling enterprises to manage adhoc manpower gaps and peak operational requirements through verified, shift-based workers.

  • Platform / facilitator model (not a traditional manpower contractor)
  • Roles: Helper, Picker, Packer, Loader, Unloader, etc.
  • All onboarding, attendance, and payouts via Web/App
  • Built on transparency, compliance, and operational ease

2. Purpose of the PILOT

  • Validate worker availability and attendance
  • Test adhoc and peak-load manpower handling
  • Validate billing, payout, and reconciliation flow

3. PILOT Timeline

  • Projected Go-Live Date: 2nd March 2026
  • Pilot Duration: 30 days
  • Scope and manpower count to be mutually agreed

4. Special PILOT Commercials

To enable a smooth proof-of-concept:

  • 100% Platform Fee Waiver
  • No operational / referral charges billed to client
  • Only Worker Payout (Net + Referral) + applicable GST will be invoiced

Post successful pilot and mutual alignment, Adhocsy will offer a preferential / discounted platform fee when the app goes fully live.

6. Payment Terms (Proposed)

  • 1st–15th working → Invoice on 16th → Payment by 30th
  • 16th–30th working → Invoice on 31st → Payment by 15th (next month)
  • 31st (if any) considered in next month cycle

Payment mode: NEFT / RTGS / IMPS from company bank account against valid tax invoice.

7. Delayed Payment Clause

  • 7-day grace period beyond due date
  • Post grace: 3% monthly interest
  • Interest invoiced at 1.5% every 15 days (cumulative basis)

8. Operational Notes

  • All worker onboarding via Adhocsy Web/App only
  • Digital attendance (start & end confirmation)
  • Per-shift micro insurance for workers during active duty
  • Adhocsy is not the employer of record
